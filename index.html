    Protect Assabet

    Dan S

    The Assabet National Wildlife Refugee protects the natural beauty of Sudbury and surrounding areas.

    It serves as a feeding and breeding ground for a variety of wildlife.

    Now Eversource wants to build high voltage power lines through the area.

    The process could greatly disturb wildlife and necessitate long-term herbicide dispersal.

    This could have catastrophic ramifications for local wildlife.

    Protect the local wildlife, and protect Sudbury. Let's get Eversource to go another way.
